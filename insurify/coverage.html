Coverage We Support

Understanding your coverage is critical to a successful claim. Insurify Claims helps policyholders navigate coverage types, limits, and exclusions—so there are no surprises.

Auto Insurance Coverage

Auto insurance coverage may apply to vehicle damage, liability, injuries, or total loss following an accident or theft.

  • ✔ Collision & comprehensive claims
  • ✔ Liability & uninsured motorist coverage
  • ✔ Rental reimbursement guidance

Homeowners Insurance Coverage

Home insurance coverage can include property damage, personal belongings, and liability depending on the policy.

  • ✔ Fire, storm, and water damage
  • ✔ Theft and vandalism claims
  • ✔ Temporary living expense support

Health Insurance Coverage

Health insurance coverage varies by plan and provider. We help clarify eligible treatments and claim requirements.

  • ✔ Hospital & medical services
  • ✔ Prescription coverage support
  • ✔ Claim denial review assistance

Business Insurance Coverage

Business policies may cover property loss, liability, or operational interruptions.

  • ✔ Commercial property damage
  • ✔ Business interruption claims
  • ✔ General & professional liability

Important Coverage Notes

Coverage availability, limits, and exclusions depend on your individual policy. Insurify Claims does not underwrite insurance policies—we provide guidance, education, and claim support to help you understand your benefits.
